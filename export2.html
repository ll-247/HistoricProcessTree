Additional Data

Integrity Levels. Each process has an integrity level: Low, Medium, High or System. A process with a lower integrity level can’t write to an object with a higher integrity level. For processes, this means that a process with low integrity level can’t open a handle with full access to a process with medium integrity level. This is what I’ll show in this post: a process with low integrity level can’t inject a DLL in a process with medium (or higher) integrity level.
